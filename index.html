About Me

Hi! I am Aditi! I am currently in grade 12. A budding poet and a bookworm (if the book isn't related to my course). If not busy jamming to music, you'll find me reflecting upon the wonders of the human psyche. Designing or Video editing is another passion of mine. I love to dance or write music in my leisure time. I am not the most athletic person but I am a pretty good swimmer and a basketball player. Organizing events is another thing I do in school and in my community. I am extremely empathetic and a methodical person who does every job thoroughly. I love interacting and connecting with new like minded people. I believe art is just science we haven’t fully understood yet. Through words, rhythm, and lines of code, I try to build meaning and beauty.

A "perfectly imperfect" combo of both EQ and IQ describes me in a nutshell.
